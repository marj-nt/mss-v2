What we can provide for you!

Babies & Children

$20 / half-hour session

Infants are slowly introduced to the water by being safely held at all times. We create a relationship with the water early by playing, swimming, and blowing bubbles! This is also a great way for infants to practice physical development skills. Pool is heated to at least 90℉.

Younger children are taught how to be safe and responsible in the water. Short distance swimming is good for children of this age. Depending on the child's skill level, we work on breathing, floating, and paddling. We want to encourage independent swimming and building good muscle memory early on!

All Ages

$25 / half-hour session

Older Children are allowed to center lessons around competitive swimming. Standard lessons are similar to those for younger children with an emphasis on safety and fundamentals.

Teens & Adults are encouraged to work on their relationship with the water at any level. This includes first-time swimmers that want to overcome fear, as well as adult swimmers that would like to improve their strokes for competition. It's never too late to learn!

Party Lifeguard

$40 / hour

Make sure that your pool party is fun and safe! Hiring a lifeguard ensures that all swimmers can enjoy themselves while someone is ready and watching the water at all times. Lifeguard is certified in First Aid, CPR, and AED.

Our Policies

Safety
Please let us know of any special needs for swimmers of any age. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Medical Conditions
  • "Bad Experiences"
  • Personal Fears

We want to help our swimmers feel safe!

Students should apply sunblock 30 minutes prior to the lesson.

Scheduling and Cancellations
Must schedule all appointments 5 days in advance.

Please be kind to let us know of any cancellations or rescheduling 24 hours in advance.
Swimming lessons - $10 charge for failing to notify within 1 hour before the lesson.
Lifeguard bookings - $20 charge for failing to notify within 24 hours before the event.

Students are welcome to add on more lessons at any time!

Payment Policy
Students are still required to pay for 7 half-hour sessions in the case of quitting early. No exceptions.

Once minimum is met, we only charge for the amount of lessons completed fully.
Payment Deadlines
Swimming lessons - every 2 weeks for lessons completed in full.
Note: First payment (of $20 or $25) is due on the first lesson.

Party lifeguard - due 1 week before the event.
